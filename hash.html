Password hash helper

Type a new family password to see its SHA-256 fingerprint. Paste that fingerprint into PASSWORD_HASH in assets/app.js and assets/review.js (and the upload Worker's UPLOAD_PASSWORD_SHA256 secret). See the README, "Changing the password."

Private by design: this runs entirely in your browser. What you type is never sent anywhere — no server, no network. You can even turn off data/Wi-Fi while using it.
